# Finance Projects

A collection of quantitative finance and financial modeling projects completed at **Tunis Business School**, spanning derivatives structuring, asset pricing, portfolio management, risk management, corporate valuation, and market microstructure.

---

## Projects

### 1. Real-Time Intrinsic Valuation of the Tunisian Dinar
**Course:** FIN460 — Dynamic Asset Pricing Theory

Hybrid stochastic model estimating the intrinsic USD/TND exchange rate in real time by combining state-space modeling with machine learning.

**Key methods:** Kalman Filter · Double Kalman Filter · Neural Network residual correction · FX basket regression  
**Results:** MAPE 0.145%, RMSE 0.0063, ρ = 0.9919 vs. interbank data — outperforming baseline ML benchmarks  

📄 [Report](./DAPT%20Project/DAPT%20Project%20Group%209.pdf)

---

### 2. Interest Rate Swap — BNA Assurances × STB Bank
**Course:** FIN330 — Derivatives

Designed and negotiated a real-world interest rate swap to hedge duration and rate-mismatch risk in a Tunisian insurance portfolio.

**Key methods:** Zero-coupon curve bootstrapping · Duration matching · IFRS 9 / BCT Circular 2021-02 compliance · Live negotiation with STB Treasury  
**Outcome:** Fully structured, signed swap contract neutralizing a −0.961 duration gap  

📄 [Report](./Derivatives%20Project/DerivativesProject-Group5.pdf)

---

### 3. Portfolio Construction & Management — Tunisian Stock Exchange
**Course:** FIN350 — Financial Markets

Built a 10-stock portfolio on the TSE using top-down macro screening and bottom-up stock selection, then optimized and actively managed it.

**Key methods:** Markowitz mean-variance optimization · CAPM · Technical analysis (SMA, MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands) · Sharpe / Sortino / VaR risk control  
**Outcome:** Outperformed the TUNINDEX benchmark through dynamic rebalancing; collaborated with Tunisie Valeurs brokerage  

📄 [Report](./Financial%20Markets%20Project/FM-ProjectReport-Group9.pdf)

---

### 4. Financial Risk Management — Tunicotex Group
**Course:** FIN460 — Financial Risk Management

End-to-end risk framework for a Tunisian industrial firm, quantifying FX, interest-rate, and liquidity exposures under normal and stressed conditions.

**Key methods:** Parametric VaR · Historical Simulation VaR · Expected Shortfall · Stress testing & scenario analysis  
**Outcome:** Identified critical tail-risk scenarios; delivered data-backed hedging recommendations to improve financial resilience  

📄 [Report](./Financial%20Risk%20Management%20Project/)

---

### 5. Corporate Valuation — Petrobras (PBR)
**Course:** Financial Modeling

Full equity valuation of Petrobras, one of the world's largest integrated energy companies, using a multi-method DCF and relative valuation framework.

**Key methods:** Three-statement financial model · DCF (WACC-based) · EV/EBITDA & P/E comps · Sensitivity analysis  
**Deliverables:** Excel financial model · Valuation report · Investor presentation  

📊 [Model](./Financial%20Modeling%20Project%20-%20Petrobras%20Valuation/Group%2019%20-%20Financial%20Model.xlsx) · 📄 [Report](./Financial%20Modeling%20Project%20-%20Petrobras%20Valuation/Group%2019%20-%20Valuation%20Report.pdf) · 📑 [Slides](./Financial%20Modeling%20Project%20-%20Petrobras%20Valuation/Group%2019%20-%20Presentation.pptx)

### 6. LOB Mid-Price Forecasting
Machine learning classification of short-horizon mid-price movements from limit order book (LOB) microstructure data.

**Key methods:** Ridge classifier · RBF-kernel SVM · Anchored walk-forward cross-validation · Real-time microstructure features
